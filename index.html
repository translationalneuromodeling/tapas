Translational Algorithms for Psychiatry-Advancing Science

About

TAPAS is a collection of software tools developed by the Translational Neuromodeling Unit (TNU) and collaborators. The goal of these tools is to support clinical neuromodeling, particularly computational psychiatry, computational neurology, and computational psychosomatics.
